Our history

This church started on 26 January 2016 in the province of Limpopo, Thohoyandou under the leadership of Pastor Ndou A and Mrs Ndou N. It started under a tree with a few members and there were no chairs. The church started with only 7 members which are the pastor,his wife,thier two children and their neighbor who surpported them in every thing that they did to make their chuch grow and gain more members. During the first year they faced a lot of challenges like on rainy days people would not attend because they would get wet if they sat under a tree, gaining more members who would love the church and work hard to mantain it.

In 2017 they build a church through organising many differnt fundraisings and during this period of time they faced a lot of challenges like the money they have raised is too little and it could not pay all the workers who were working but at the end of the year the church was finished.This time they did not have to worry about rain and some bad weather neacuse the church was fineshed and they also bought some chairs for anyone who was comig to attend church. Since 2024 they were busy with building a bigger church that it is going to fit their members in it.
